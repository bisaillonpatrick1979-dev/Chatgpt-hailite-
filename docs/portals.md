# Portals

The app has two main portal families.

## Admin portal

Owner and admin users can see company settings, workers, projects, clients, documents, invoices, payroll, reports and AI configuration.

## Worker portal

Employees and subcontractors can see only their own profile, assigned projects, assigned tasks, punch records, production reports, pay balance and personal documents.

Workers must not see company finances, admin dashboards, other workers, unrelated projects or profit reports.

## AI layer

The AI layer is provider neutral. It can connect to OpenAI, Gemini or Claude through an adapter. The selected onboarding region controls currency, taxes, unit system and construction context.

Alberta uses CAD, GST and imperial units. Texas uses USD and imperial units. Europe uses EUR, VAT and metric units.
